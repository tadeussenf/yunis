The Sound of the Earth

In the space between thoughts, there is a vibration. My music is not just heard, but felt—a bridge between the ancient wisdom of the mountains and the gentle breath of the forest.

Using the primal resonance of the Tam Tam Gong, I craft soundscapes for yoga, deep meditation, and communal healing. Each performance is a painting in sound, textured with the colors of dusk and the peace of nature.

Deep Meditation

Like the stillness of a mountain peak at dusk, these sessions are designed to ground your spirit. The low frequencies of the gong resonate with the root chakra, creating a profound sense of safety and stability.
